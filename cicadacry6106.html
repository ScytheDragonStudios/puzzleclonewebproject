This is a project for CodeKentucky, a school program to help break people into tech.

Cicada3031 was a puzzle site, first revealed back in 2007.
The last puzzle was released in 2017, but no one knows who exactly cicada was or what they wanted.
A CIA recruitment trick? Hacktivists looking for talent? Anonymous? Speculations fly.

Purpose of this site

The purpose of this site is 2 fold

  1. To showcase what I'm learning in CodeLouisville
  2. To create something I've always wanted to make, a literal website game (puzzle)

I'm not much of a 'Design' person, I am much more comfortable in the back-end part of coding, with C++ or C#.

I sincerely hope you enjoy the logic puzzles, and as I learn I plan to come back and keep improving this site.
